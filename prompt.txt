1. YEAR NAVIGATION — ANIMATED FOCUS
When the user clicks a year in the top bar navigation:
1. Calculate the x position of that year's January 1st on the canvas
   targetX = yearIndex * 1440 * zoom (1440px per year)

2. Animate the canvas world transform smoothly:
   Duration: 700ms
   Easing: easeInOutCubic
   Both x (pan) and scale animate simultaneously if needed
   The year's content should end up centered in the viewport
   accounting for left panel (240px) and right panel (200px)

3. During animation:
   - Year label in top bar updates immediately on click
   - Active year underline slides to the new year with a
     200ms CSS transition (transform: translateX)
   - Canvas cards that are entering the viewport fade in
     as they cross the left panel edge (opacity 0→1, 300ms)
   - Cards leaving the viewport fade out as they cross right panel edge

4. After animation lands:
   - Flash the year watermark: rgba(255,255,255,0.04) → rgba(255,255,255,0.1)
     then back to rgba(255,255,255,0.04) over 600ms
   - Show toast: "Viewing 2024 · {N} ventures"

2. CLICK-TO-FOCUS NAVIGATION
Clicking ANYTHING that references a venture triggers a smooth canvas navigation to that venture.
Triggers:

Clicking a venture name in the left panel portfolio list
Clicking a venture row in the LIST overlay
Clicking an event in the right panel live feed
Clicking a branch label on the canvas
Using ← → keyboard arrows to navigate between ventures

Animation spec:
1. Calculate target transform:
   targetX = ventureCard.worldX - (viewportWidth - leftPanel - rightPanel) / 2
   targetY = ventureCard.worldY - viewportHeight / 2 + 80
   targetScale = clamp(currentScale, 0.8, 1.2)
   — don't zoom in too much, just center it

2. Animate world container:
   Duration: 650ms
   Easing: easeInOutCubic
   Interpolate x, y, scale simultaneously

3. On arrival:
   — Select the venture card (triggers white outline + glow)
   — Card performs a subtle "attention" animation:
     scale 1.0 → 1.03 → 1.0 over 400ms, easeInOutSine
   — The dot on the timeline pulses:
     radius 5 → 9 → 5 over 500ms with a fading ring expanding outward
     ring: circle growing from radius 5 to 24, alpha 0.4→0, strokeWidth 1px
   — Left panel: highlight that venture's row

4. If the venture is in a different year than currently viewed:
   — First snap the year selector to the correct year (no animation for year label)
   — Then run the canvas pan animation
   — Show toast: "FreightOS Node · Jun 2024"
